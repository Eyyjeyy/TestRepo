About Us

Unleash is more than just a pet app - it's a vibrant community dedicated to celebrating the lives of our furry (and feathery!) friends. Founded by pet lovers, this all-in-one platform combines fun features like e-commerce, social networking, entertainment, and essential information of various pet activities that pet owners need to know.

Unleash aims to connect pet lovers worldwide, building a safe and happy haven for both pets and their humans. Join the pack and discover a world where your pet's well-being and joy are unleashed!

  • Express yourself and document your pet's journey! Create profiles, share unlimited photos and videos, and engage in discussions about various pet-related topics.

  • Reach out to more than 10,000 pet products and services according to your location

  • Connect with other pet lovers and have your own virtual pet in a thriving community.

  • Keep updated with the latest news, trends, events, and any information you need to know for your furbaby.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I setup an account?

    1. Download the Unleash app through Google Play Store (for Android users) or the Apple Store (for iOS users).

    2. Register with your email address, or log in directly with your Facebook ID, Google ID or Apple ID.

    3. Enter your Full Name, Birthdate, Gender, and Age.

  • How can I retrieve my account if I forgot my password?

    To recover your password, go to the email login screen and click ‚ÄúForgot Password‚Äù. Enter your registered email address and wait for the email with the link to reset your password

  • How do I send treats to pets/user profiles?

    To send treats, you will need to buy Unleash Bits.

  • How do I purchase Unleash Bits?

    To purchase Unleash Bits,tap the treats Icon, tap Bits Balance and choose the amount you'd like to purchase.

  • How do I pay for Unleash Bits?

    You can pay via Apple Pay for IOS or Google Pay for Android devices.

MERCHANTS

  • How to register as a partner-merchant?

    To register as a partner-merchant, you may send an email to support@unleash.ph or call us at +63 917 119 8290.

  • How is the pay-out process?

    Seller payouts are paid via bank transfer to the account you have provided when you signed up as a partner-merchant. Please note that the actual crediting of funds in your bank account depends on your bank's processing time.

  • What is the refund and return policy?

    1. Customers must contact our customer service team at support@unleash.ph to initiate a return/refund request.

    2. Provide the order number, product details, and reason for the return/refund request.

    3. Our team will guide customers through the return process if applicable.
